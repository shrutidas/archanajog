About

Archana Joglekar is a prominent Indian actress and renowned classical dancer. She has acted in Hindi, Marathi, Odia and Bengali films and television serials. Some of her noted films are Sansar (Hindi), Eka Peksha Ek (Marathi) and Anapekshit (Marathi). Trained in Kathak by her mother, Srimati Guru Asha Joglekar, Archana has carried on the legacy of Archana Nrityala in the US. She is the founder and director of Archana Arts.
